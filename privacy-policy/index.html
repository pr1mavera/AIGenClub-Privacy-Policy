Introduction

AIGenClub ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Pinterest application "AIGenClub Publisher" and the website primavera76.gumroad.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with our Pinterest application.

Information We Access

Our Pinterest application accesses only the following information from the AIGenClub business account:

  • Boards: We read our own board names and IDs to organize pins.

We do not access, collect, or store any personal data from other Pinterest users.

How We Use Information

The sole purpose of our application is to automatically publish marketing pins for our educational digital products (math worksheets) to our own Pinterest boards, driving traffic to our Gumroad store. We do not share any data with third parties.

Data Storage and Security

We store Pinterest API tokens securely. No personal user data is collected, stored, or processed by our application.

Third-Party Services

Our application uses:

Each service has its own privacy policy governing data handling.
